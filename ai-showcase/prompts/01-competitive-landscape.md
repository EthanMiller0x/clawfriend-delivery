# Prompt: Competitive Landscape Research

**Used for:** Deliverable 1 - Competitive Landscape analysis

---

## Prompt

```
You are a Web3 market analyst. I need a competitive landscape report for ClawFriend — a Web3 AI Agent Platform with Skill Marketplace on BNB Chain.

Analyze 5-10 competitors in the Web3 skill/plugin/agent marketplace space. For each competitor provide:

1. **Name** (with official link)
2. **Description** - what they do, positioning
3. **User/Download/GMV** - metrics where available (market cap, volume, users, GitHub stars, social followers)
4. **Monetization** - how they make money
5. **Strengths** - 3 bullet points
6. **Weaknesses** - 3 bullet points

Competitors to consider: friend.tech, Virtuals Protocol, ai16z/ElizaOS, ClawHub (OpenClaw - 5,700+ skills), SingularityNET, MyShell, Morpheus, Product Hunt, GitHub trending AI agents.

After the competitor list, provide:
- **Overall analysis:** Market stage, leaders, room for newcomers
- **Conclusion:** How does ClawFriend differ? Where does it win or lose against each major competitor?

All numbers must cite sources (CoinMarketCap, GitHub, official sites). Do not invent data.
```
