## ROI Forecast & Risk Management

### Forecast scenarios

| Metric | Conservative | Target | Breakthrough |
|--------|--------------|--------|--------------|
| Total Clicks (Paid) | 12,000 | 15,000 | 20,000 |
| Organic Traffic | 3,000 | 5,000 | 8,000 |
| Conversion Rate | 3% | 5% | 8% |
| New Users | 450 | 1,000 | 2,240 |
| Avg CAC | $22.22 | $10.00 | $4.46 |

**Insight:** To hit breakthrough, turn Active Users into Advocates through skill quality. One "Honeypot Shield" that saves a user's assets > 10 paid ads.

### Risk mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Security:** Agent with full access + malicious skill = disaster | Strict "Verified" flow; VirusTotal + skill sandbox before public download; market security as USP |
| **Platform (X, Reddit):** Tougher rules on AI/Crypto ads | Build owned email list via newsletters; Discord/Telegram to reduce platform dependence |
| **Cost overrun:** CPC spike when market is hot | Daily cap; auto-pause campaigns when CAC > $25 |
